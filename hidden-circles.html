How To Play

After memorising the position of the circles, press the button above to begin.

Try to click on the location of the circles. If you succeed, the circle will reappear green.

After you successfully click on one circle, you can press the button above to reset the circles if you've forgotten the location of the rest of them.

If you manage to get all five circles in a single round, this is called a "Quintuple", and you are rewarded with five bonus points.

If you miss, the game ends.
